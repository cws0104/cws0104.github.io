\### üß™ Types of Acoustofluidic Holography (AFH)



\#### 1. **General AFH (Continuous Phase Kinoform)**

\#### 2. **Binary AFH**

\- Result: Forms **compact cell patterns**, **fiber structures** with predefined shapes
